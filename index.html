Let's Play Mastermind

Objective

Guess the secret code!
(Hint: There are no duplicate colors)

Instructions

  1. Drag the pegs into the slots on the game board
  2. Based on your code, the game will provide clues:
    • Black: Correct color and position
    • White: Correct color, wrong position
    • Blank: Wrong color
  3. Too tough? Click 'Reveal Answer'
